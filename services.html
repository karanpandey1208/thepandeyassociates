Welcome

Pandey Associates is a dynamic and innovative startup tax consultancy firm that specializes in providing expert tax guidance and solutions to businesses of all sizes. Founded by experienced tax professionals, Pandey Associates aims to simplify the intricate landscape of tax regulations and empower businesses to make informed financial decisions.

Introducing the Tax Maestro: Mr. Shekhar Pandey, a seasoned tax consultant with over two decades of experience, has honed his skills to perfection. He is your one-stop destination for all your financial and taxation needs. His profound expertise covers a multitude of services, ensuring that individuals and businesses can navigate the complex financial terrain with ease.

    Services Offered

  • GST Registration: Whether you're an entrepreneur launching a startup or an established business entity, Arvind Kumar can assist you in navigating the labyrinth of GST registration.
  • Income Tax Return (ITR) Filing: With tax laws continually evolving, ensuring that you comply with all the regulations while optimizing your returns can be a daunting task. Mr. Kumar's expertise ensures that your ITR is filed accurately and efficiently.
  • Sale Tax Return: Understanding and complying with sales tax regulations is crucial for businesses of all sizes. Arvind Kumar helps businesses file their sale tax returns accurately and on time.
  • GST Return: Staying up to date with your Goods and Services Tax (GST) returns is essential. Mr. Kumar's team will ensure that your GST returns are filed correctly, helping you avoid penalties and compliance issues.
  • PAN Card Application: Need a Permanent Account Number (PAN) card? Arvind Kumar streamlines the application process, making it hassle-free for individuals and businesses.
  • Book Keeping: Accurate bookkeeping is the foundation of financial stability. Arvind Kumar's team can maintain your financial records with precision, helping you make informed financial decisions.
  • TDS Return: Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) can be a confusing process. Mr. Kumar ensures that your TDS returns are filed accurately, reducing your tax liabilities.
  • New DSC Token: With the increasing importance of digital signatures, Mr. Kumar can assist in obtaining a new Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) token, facilitating secure digital transactions.
  • DIN Registration: Directors Identification Number (DIN) registration is vital for company directors. Arvind Kumar simplifies this process, ensuring compliance with corporate governance regulations.
  • Company Registration: From the inception of a business idea to the legal establishment of a company, Arvind Kumar guides you through every step of the company registration process.
  • Tax Consultancy: Whether you need advice on tax planning, investment, or compliance, Mr. Kumar provides expert tax consultancy services tailored to your unique financial situation.
  • All Types of Compliance in Accounts: Staying compliant with financial regulations can be a logistical nightmare. Mr. Kumar's team ensures that you adhere to all financial and tax-related compliance requirements.
